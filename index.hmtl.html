Who We Are

Aman Singh Rathore is the visionary founder of Marcadors, bringing over 10 years of rich industry experience to the forefront. Known for his strategic thinking and solution-oriented approach, Aman has consistently delivered impactful results across diverse domains. Under his leadership, Marcadors has not only thrived in the competitive private sector but has also successfully executed multiple government projects, earning a reputation for reliability and innovation. His deep understanding of market dynamics, combined with a passion for technology and design, has positioned him as a trusted name in the industry. Aman believes in creating value through purposeful collaboration, sharp execution, and a forward-thinking mindset. With a track record of excellence and a clear vision for the future, Aman Singh Rathore continues to drive Marcadors towards new horizons of growth and impact.
